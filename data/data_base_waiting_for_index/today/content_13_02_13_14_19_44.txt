WMIADAP.EXE

WMIADAP.EXE is a process which forms a part of the Microsoft Windows Operating System. The AutoDiscovery/AutoPurge ( ADAP) process transfers performance libraries to the WMI repository. This program is important for the stable and secure running of your computer。
